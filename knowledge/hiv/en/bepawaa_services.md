# Bepawaa HIV Services

Source notes: Internal service documentation for Bepawaa platform.

## What Bepawaa Offers for HIV Support

Bepawaa is a health platform that connects you with pharmacies, labs, and support services — all with privacy and dignity.

## Available HIV Services

### 1. HIV Self-Test Kits
- Order approved HIV self-test kits from verified pharmacies
- Discreet, plain packaging — no one knows what's inside
- Cash on Delivery (COD) available — pay when you receive
- Delivered to your preferred location
- How to order: Browse products → Select HIV self-test kit → Choose delivery → Pay on delivery

### 2. PrEP Consultations
- Book a consultation with a PrEP provider through the platform
- Find which pharmacies and clinics offer PrEP near you
- Get information about starting, continuing, or stopping PrEP
- Follow-up support and prescription management

### 3. PEP Access (Emergency)
- Find the nearest facility providing PEP
- Understand the urgency (must start within 72 hours of exposure)
- Book an urgent appointment
- Get step-by-step guidance through the process

### 4. Lab Testing
- Book HIV lab tests at verified laboratories
- Results delivered securely through the platform
- Confirmatory testing after a reactive self-test
- CD4 count, viral load, and other monitoring tests

### 5. Emotional Support (Bepawaa Care)
- Confidential AI-powered counseling available 24/7
- Support for stigma, anxiety, fear, and emotional wellbeing
- Guidance through testing, treatment, and disclosure decisions
- Available in English and Kiswahili
- Access via web chat or WhatsApp

### 6. Provider Directory
- Find pharmacies that stock HIV test kits
- Locate labs offering HIV-related tests
- Discover clinics providing PrEP and PEP services
- View operating hours, distance, and services

## How COD (Cash on Delivery) Works

1. Browse and select products (e.g., HIV self-test kit)
2. Choose Cash on Delivery as payment method
3. Enter your delivery address (location picker ensures accuracy)
4. A rider picks up your order from the pharmacy
5. You pay the rider in cash when you receive the package
6. Package is in plain, discreet packaging

## Privacy Commitment

- All orders are packaged discreetly
- Chat conversations are confidential
- Your health data is protected
- No one is notified about your purchases or inquiries
- You control who sees your information
